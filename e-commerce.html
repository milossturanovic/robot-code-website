OUR SERVICES

E-commerce and online store

We are a web agency in Gothenburg that develops professional e-commerce and online stores with systems that streamline our customers’ workflows!

E-commerce is incredibly common today, but many businesses struggle to create an effective website where e-commerce flows smoothly. This can be due to many things, but mainly that they don’t have the tools to create an efficient and fast service that is easy for customers to use.

With our help, you too can have a fast, efficient and technologically optimised e-commerce experience that your customers will love. That’s because we develop e-commerce and online stores from the ground up, optimising them with system integrations that streamline your daily workflow.

How do we work with e-commerce?

We can take care of everything for you - from idea to launch. With smart solutions and technical expertise, we can make your vision a reality. We do this in three simple steps.

We create professional websites that are tailored to each client's wishes and needs. We also take care of the technical side, so that your website performs to today's exacting standards.

We plan your project

Planning is the cornerstone of any project, and not least when it comes to e-commerce. We’ll work with you to create ideas, run workshops and find strategies that will get you far.

We implement the project

Once we’ve planned the project, it’s time to let us take over and do what we do best. From design to system integration and technical maintenance, we’ll take care of all the necessary steps.

We manage and monitor

Once your e-commerce is up and running, we take care of the operation and management. We’ll also follow up on how you’re doing and if there’s anything we can do to further optimise the site.

We develop secure e-commerce with smart integrations

As well as working, having short load times and being optimised for the visitor, your e-commerce needs to be safe to use. Secure e-commerce that offers safe payment options and insurance for you as a manager is important to make your work run as smoothly as possible.

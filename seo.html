Our work with SEO

We’re experts in all things SEO, whether it’s search engine optimising content, analysing trends and developing the right keywords for your industry, keyword advertising or just getting you seen on Google. We know the industry, we know the strategies and we know

Analysis.

In order to succeed with your search engine optimisation, you first need to carry out analyses. At Robotcode, we analyse your website and create a report based on how you are doing, which keywords you are using and which you should be using. We analyse your content, your website and your other channels and give you a basis for your current ranking.

Strategies.

All forms of digital marketing need to be supported by strategies. This is something we at Robotcode know about, and something we work on for all our clients. Based on the analysis, we develop a tailored strategy that we then work from throughout the process.

Objectives and KPIs.

Your search engine optimisation needs to have a goal. Do you want to rank high on Google? Do you want to get more out of your ads? Do you want to be seen locally? We’ll work together to develop goals for your business and find the right KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) to base our work on.

Search Engine Optimization

Once we’ve done our analysis, developed a strategy and set sustainable goals - that’s when the work on search engine optimisation begins. We write content, optimise your website, advertise and monitor results - all in close collaboration with you, of course.

Local SEO

Are you interested in gaining a lot of visibility in your immediate area? Many companies actively target customers in their immediate vicinity, and want to optimise their website and content primarily to reach a target audience in their local area. With local SEO, we can target this specific audience - so you reach the right people, at the right time.

Reports

Of course, search engine optimisation needs to be monitored, so you know our techniques are working and you’re getting the results you want. We use your KPIs and produce reports that show you where you are, how close you are to your goal and how your visibility has changed since you started.

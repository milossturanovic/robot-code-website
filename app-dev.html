our process

Create an app in three steps

When we develop apps, we combine our common skills to create something unique all the time. Together with our clients, we develop a plan, where everything from design to system integrations is reviewed to create a blueprint for the entire process. We are also with you throughout the process, from start to finish. When we help our clients to design, you could say that we work in three stages.

Planning and ideation

It all starts with an idea and that’s where you come in! Our clients come to us with an idea and a vision - we make it a reality. To begin the project, we take your ideas and develop a plan based on your requirements. We create a requirements specification and develop wireframes, as well as help you develop the right features your app needs.

Design and features

All successful apps have a combination of good design, ease of use and functional, technical solutions. To create a user-friendly and accessible design, we generate ideas based on your business idea and produce wireframes for you to approve. We include all the features necessary for the purpose of the mobile application and we optimise it to work as well as it can.

Launch.

An app is more than just good design - it should also work properly. Using the latest technologies, such as Kotlin and Swift, we create sustainable and smart solutions that are tailored to your app and ensure that everything works properly at launch. We’re involved in the whole process - from the analysis and design phase to implementation. We’ll also be with you once the launch is complete to make sure everything works as it should, and help you with any future maintenance if you need it.
